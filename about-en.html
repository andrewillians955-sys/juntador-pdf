About

This portal was created to address a recurring need: democratizing simple productivity tools that help users organize tasks and documents with confidence. We understand that many people need practical utilities to merge PDFs, manage attachments, and prepare documents for everyday workflows— both in academic and professional contexts.

1. Mission

Our mission is to make productivity easier through tools that are transparent, easy to use, and respectful of user privacy. We aim to reduce friction for students, office professionals, and organizations that rely on PDFs to complete everyday tasks. The goal is to ensure that users can perform their work efficiently while maintaining clear expectations about how the tool operates.

2. Vision

Our vision is to build a set of reliable, free, and safe tools that prioritize privacy and responsible development. We intend for trust to be built through consistency in technical practices, transparent notices, and mechanisms that support informed decisions, including cookie consent experiences when advertising is present.

3. Privacy values: client-side processing

3.1 Local processing as a core principle

We prioritize privacy through client-side technology. The merging process is executed in the user’s browser, which reduces the need for uploading documents to our infrastructure and lowers exposure associated with transfers and server storage. This approach supports a more controlled experience while keeping the tool focused on its primary purpose.

3.2 Transparency and user control

We believe users should have accessible information about cookies, consent choices, and relevant policies. Transparency is essential so the user understands how advertising may be served and how preferences can be managed in the browser environment.

4. Maintaining free access through non-intrusive advertising

The portal remains free through a sustainable advertising model. We aim for a less intrusive approach, always within the boundaries of applicable consent requirements. The intention is to continue providing utility without compromising core principles of privacy and compliance.
