The Ultimate Tool to Merge Free PDF

Our Online PDF Merger was created to make your day easier. Whether you are a student organizing assignments, a lawyer compiling case files, or an office professional handling reports, our tool lets you merge multiple documents into a single file quickly and intuitively.

Our biggest advantage is absolute privacy. Unlike most online tools that require you to upload your documents to third-party servers, our system uses client-side technology. That means the entire merge process happens directly in the memory of your own browser. Because all operations are executed on your device, third-party exposure is minimized and your documents remain protected throughout the entire process. until you download the PDF file.

How to use the tool (Step by Step)

  1. Send your files: Drag and drop your PDFs into the marked area or click the button to select them.
  2. Arrange the order: Click and hold any file in the list, then drag it up or down to set the exact sequence.
  3. Merge the PDFs: Click the main "Merge PDFs" button and wait for the progress bar.
  4. Download: Click "Download PDF" to save the new document to your device.
